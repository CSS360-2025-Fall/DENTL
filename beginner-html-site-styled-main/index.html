DENTL — Your Discord Gambling Companion

DENTL is a fun and educational Discord bot designed to simulate gambling systems using virtual credits — no real money involved.

How DENTL Works

  • Daily Credits: Claim virtual money every day to grow your account balance.
  • Game Rules: Ask DENTL for instructions anytime — it knows every game it offers.
  • Responsible Play: Feeling lucky? There’s even a playful 1-in-6 “kick” command for those wanting to take a break.
  • Multilingual Support: DENTL speaks multiple languages so everyone can play together.
  • Virtual Stock Market: Invest your fake credits in a simulated market for high-stakes fun.
  • Win–Loss Tracking: Keep score of your performance and show off your stats.
