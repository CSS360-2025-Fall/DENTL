How DENTL Works

DENTL gives every player a virtual account and lets them try out casino-style systems in a safe environment. You earn credits, make bets, and watch your balance rise and fall — all without risking real money.

Daily Credits

Claim free credits once a day to grow your starting bankroll. Great for testing new strategies.

Game Rules on Demand

Unsure how a game works? Ask the bot for the rules before you play so you always know what you are doing.

Responsible Play

Need a break? Use the playful 1-in-6 “kick” style commands or take advantage of betting limits for a more realistic feel.

Multilingual Support

Servers with friends from different countries can all join in. DENTL supports multiple languages.

Virtual Stock Market

Invest credits into a fake stock market that updates regularly. Watch your “portfolio” go up or down.

Win–Loss Tracking

Track wins, losses, win rate, and biggest bets to see how your skills evolve over time.

Games in DENTL

DENTL includes a variety of casino-style games and interactive systems. Click a game below to see how it works inside the bot.

Blackjack

Beat the dealer without going over 21.

Command: /blackjack

Plays against the casino. Uses your DENTL balance for bets and updates your stats with wins, losses, and ties.

Poker (Planned)

A full poker system is being developed for Version 2.0.0, offering multi-round betting and hand comparisons.

Planned for Version 2.0.0. Players will be able to join tables, bet through multiple rounds, and compare final hands.

Rock, Paper, Scissors

Quick bets against the casino or friends.

Command: /rps

Easy way to test probability and streaks. Great starter game when you first join the bot.

Coin Flip

A classic 50/50 gamble with simple payouts.

Command: /coinflip

Helps players see how randomness can still feel “unfair” over short runs even when the odds are even.

Russian Roulette

Risky high-stakes game with a playful twist.

Command: /russianroulette

Simulates a one-in-six chance event. Good for exploring risk and reward without real danger.

Stock Market

Invest in changing prices and watch your credits move.

Command: /stock and related commands

DENTL includes a virtual stock market where prices change over time and players can invest their credits to try to grow their balance.

FAQ

Is this real gambling?

No. All credits in DENTL are virtual and have no real-world value. The bot is meant to explore probability, risk, and reward in a safe way.

Can I reset my stats or balance?

Server admins can configure how resets and wipes work. Typically, there are commands to reset balances or start fresh seasons for leaderboards.

Where can I learn more?

Check out the official documentation or the GitHub repository for technical details.
